Account Deletion Request – AndChess

If you would like to delete your AndChess account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to: ampersandchess@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: “Account Deletion Request”
  3. Include the email address / lichess user_name used to log into the app
We will process your request and delete your account within 5–7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed, we will delete your account and associated data, including:

  • User profile information
  • Linked accounts (such as Google or Lichess, if connected)
  • Game data and activity
  • Attendance records and related activity
  • In-app progress such as XP, coins, and usage data

Data Retention

Certain minimal data may be retained for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes, as required by applicable laws.
